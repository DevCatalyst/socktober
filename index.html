Did you know that socks are the least donated items to most homeless shelters?

Every night in the United States, an estimated 600,000 people live on the streets. This October, we want 2 million people to prove that even a small act of love, such as donating a pair of socks, can make big difference in the lives of our neighbors who are homeless.

Socktober is an easy and fun movement to help the homeless in your community during the month of October. Kid President creator Brad Montague launched Socktober as a way to get socks and other necessities to the homeless.

Kid President and Grover get the entire world involved in Socktober!
